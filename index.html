Initially, our project was designed to be a wind tunnel used to demonstrate the principles behind flight. It was originally comprised of four systems, each controlling a different part of the wind tunnel and communicating with each other via a shared UART line. Unfortunately due to several factors, two of our group members had to leave our group in the last few weeks of class, before the project could be fully debugged and assembled. Of the two remaining members, only one was able to get their board working, that being the system that recieves the user input. Pictured below is the human interface system connected to an ESP32 on a breadboard for the purose of demonstrating inter-system communication functionality.
